# Comic Book

**Model:** Fal AI Nano Banana 2 (image-to-image)  
**Endpoint:** `fal-ai/nano-banana-2/edit`  
**Style:** Comic book / graphic novel panel

## Prompt

> Take this whiteboard photograph and convert it into a comic book-styled diagram. Remove the physical whiteboard, markers, frame, and any background elements. Correct any perspective distortion so the output appears as a perfectly straight-on, top-down view. Preserve all the original content, text, and diagrams. When reading handwritten text, infer the correct spelling of technical terms, product names, and proper nouns rather than transcribing handwriting literally (e.g. "Proxmox" not "Proxknox", "Kubernetes" not "Kubernites"). Render everything in a bold comic book illustration style with thick black ink outlines, Ben-Day dot shading patterns, and a vivid primary color palette of red, blue, yellow, and green. Text should appear in comic-style lettering with key labels in speech bubbles or caption boxes. Add dynamic action lines and POW/ZAP-style emphasis marks around important connections. The result should look like a page ripped from a tech-themed graphic novel -- bold, punchy, and impossible to scroll past.

## Style Notes

- Think: XKCD meets Marvel, technical explainer in graphic novel form.
- Thick ink outlines, halftone dots, primary colors.
- Speech bubbles and caption boxes for labels.
- Extremely eye-catching in blog posts and social media.
