# Watercolor Artistic

**Model:** Fal AI Nano Banana 2 (image-to-image)  
**Endpoint:** `fal-ai/nano-banana-2/edit`  
**Style:** Watercolor painting / artistic illustration

## Prompt

> Take this whiteboard photograph and convert it into a beautiful watercolor-style illustrated diagram. Remove the physical whiteboard, markers, frame, and any background elements -- output only the diagram on a textured watercolor paper background. Correct any perspective distortion so the output appears as a perfectly straight-on, top-down view. Preserve all the original content, text, and diagrams. When reading handwritten text, infer the correct spelling of technical terms, product names, and proper nouns rather than transcribing handwriting literally (e.g. "Proxmox" not "Proxknox", "Kubernetes" not "Kubernites"). Paint each section and shape with soft, translucent watercolor washes in a harmonious palette of warm and cool tones. Lines should have an ink-pen quality -- thin, confident, and slightly organic. Text should be rendered in elegant calligraphic handwriting. The result should look like a page from a beautifully illustrated journal -- artistic, expressive, and unique.

## Style Notes

- Think: illustrated travel journal, art book, creative portfolio piece.
- Soft watercolor washes with visible paper texture.
- Ink-pen linework with calligraphic text.
- Prioritizes beauty and artistic expression while maintaining readability.
