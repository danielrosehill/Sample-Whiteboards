## Prompt

> Take this whiteboard photograph and convert it into a stark, minimalist black-and-white diagram. Remove the physical whiteboard, markers, frame, and any background elements -- output only the diagram on a pure white background. Correct any perspective distortion so the output appears as a perfectly straight-on, top-down view. Preserve all the original content, text, and diagrams. When reading handwritten text, infer the correct spelling of technical terms, product names, and proper nouns rather than transcribing handwriting literally (e.g. "Proxmox" not "Proxknox", "Kubernetes" not "Kubernites"). Use only black lines, shapes, and text -- no color, no gradients, no fills. Lines should be clean and uniform weight. Use a simple, elegant sans-serif font. Maximize whitespace and let the structure breathe. The result should be austere and elegant -- like a diagram in an academic paper or a Dieter Rams design specification.
