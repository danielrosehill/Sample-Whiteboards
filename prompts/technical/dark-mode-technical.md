# Dark Mode Technical

**Model:** Fal AI Nano Banana 2 (image-to-image)  
**Endpoint:** `fal-ai/nano-banana-2/edit`  
**Style:** Dark background, technical/engineering aesthetic

## Prompt

> Take this whiteboard photograph and convert it into a technical diagram with a dark background aesthetic. Remove the physical whiteboard, markers, frame, and any background elements -- output the diagram on a dark charcoal or near-black background. Correct any perspective distortion so the output appears as a perfectly straight-on, top-down view. Preserve all the original content, text, and diagrams. When reading handwritten text, infer the correct spelling of technical terms, product names, and proper nouns rather than transcribing handwriting literally (e.g. "Proxmox" not "Proxknox", "Kubernetes" not "Kubernites"). Use light-colored text (white or light grey) and neon or high-contrast accent colors like cyan, green, and orange for lines, arrows, and highlights. Use a monospace or technical font for labels. Add subtle grid lines or dot patterns in the background. The result should look like a technical blueprint or engineering schematic -- precise, detailed, and developer-friendly.

## Style Notes

- Think: architecture diagram in a dev tool, terminal aesthetic, dark-mode documentation.
- Dark background with high-contrast elements -- easy on the eyes.
- Monospace typography, sharp edges, thin precise lines.
- Grid or dot-matrix background adds subtle structure.
