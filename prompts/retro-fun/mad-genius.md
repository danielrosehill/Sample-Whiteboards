# Mad Genius

**Model:** Fal AI Nano Banana 2 (image-to-image)  
**Endpoint:** `fal-ai/nano-banana-2/edit`  
**Style:** Chaotic genius / beautiful mind

## Prompt

> Take this whiteboard photograph and convert it into a chaotic, sprawling mad-genius-style diagram. Remove the physical whiteboard, markers, frame, and any background elements -- output on a slightly yellowed, aged paper background with coffee stain marks and creased folds. Correct any perspective distortion so the output appears as a perfectly straight-on, top-down view. Preserve all the original content, text, and diagrams. When reading handwritten text, infer the correct spelling of technical terms, product names, and proper nouns rather than transcribing handwriting literally (e.g. "Proxmox" not "Proxknox", "Kubernetes" not "Kubernites"). Amplify the chaos -- make the handwriting more frantic and varied in size, add scribbled annotations in margins, underline things multiple times, circle key concepts aggressively, draw arrows that loop and cross over each other. Use red ink for emphatic corrections and blue ink for the main content. Scatter small doodles, question marks, and exclamation points in empty spaces. The result should look like the notebook of a brilliant but unhinged mind -- feverish, obsessive, and crackling with intellectual energy.

## Style Notes

- Think: John Nash's office in A Beautiful Mind, conspiracy theory wall, inventor's notebook.
- Aged paper, multiple ink colors, frantic layered annotations.
- Controlled chaos -- it looks wild but all original content is preserved.
- Conveys urgency, obsession, and raw intellectual energy.
