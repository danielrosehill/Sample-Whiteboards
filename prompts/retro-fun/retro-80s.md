# Retro 80s

**Model:** Fal AI Nano Banana 2 (image-to-image)  
**Endpoint:** `fal-ai/nano-banana-2/edit`  
**Style:** Retro 1980s synthwave / neon

## Prompt

> Take this whiteboard photograph and convert it into a retro 1980s synthwave-styled diagram. Remove the physical whiteboard, markers, frame, and any background elements -- output the diagram on a dark purple-to-black gradient background. Correct any perspective distortion so the output appears as a perfectly straight-on, top-down view. Preserve all the original content, text, and diagrams. When reading handwritten text, infer the correct spelling of technical terms, product names, and proper nouns rather than transcribing handwriting literally (e.g. "Proxmox" not "Proxknox", "Kubernetes" not "Kubernites"). Use hot pink, electric cyan, and neon yellow for lines, shapes, and text. Add subtle scan-line effects and a retro grid perspective floor fading into the background. Use a bold, blocky retro font for all labels. The result should feel like a tech diagram from an 80s sci-fi movie -- glowing, vibrant, and unmistakably retro-futuristic.

## Style Notes

- Think: Tron, synthwave album cover, retro arcade aesthetic.
- Neon glow effects on lines and text.
- Dark gradient background with perspective grid.
- Fun and nostalgic while still communicating the diagram content clearly.
